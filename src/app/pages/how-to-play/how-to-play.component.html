How to Play Hitster Online

musicguessr lets you play the Hitster card game in your browser — using YouTube, Spotify, or Apple Music instead of being locked to one service. Here's how to get started.

What you need

  • 🃏A physical Hitster card game set
  • 📱A smartphone or tablet with a camera
  • ▶ For YouTube: nothing — it's free with no login
  • ♫For Spotify: a Spotify Premium subscription
  • For Apple Music: an Apple Music subscription

Step-by-step guide

  1. 1 Open musicguessr in your browser

    Go to musicguessr.app in Safari, Chrome, or any modern browser. No download or installation needed.

  2. 2 Choose your music service

    Tap Start Playing and pick a provider. Choose YouTube for free play with no login. Spotify and Apple Music require a subscription. Your choice is locked for the entire game session.

  3. 3 Allow camera access

    When prompted, allow the browser to use your camera. This is needed to scan QR codes on Hitster cards. musicguessr never stores photos or video.

  4. 4 Scan a Hitster card

    Point your camera at the QR code printed on the back of any Hitster card. The scanner reads it automatically — no button press needed. The track is identified within a second.

  5. 5 Tap to play

    A "Tap to Play" overlay appears. Tap it — the song starts in your browser immediately. The card details (title, artist, year, artwork) are blurred until you reveal them.

    iPhone tip: iOS Safari requires a direct tap to start audio. Always tap the overlay rather than trying to play automatically.

  6. 6 Listen and guess the year

    Let the song play and discuss with your group. When everyone has guessed, tap Tap to reveal to unblur the card and see the release year.

  7. 7 Scan the next card

    Tap Scan next card to return to the scanner. Repeat for every card in your round. The player who places the most cards correctly on the timeline wins.

Playing with custom decks

Don't have Hitster cards? You can create your own music quiz deck from any YouTube videos or playlist.

  1. 1 Go to Create Deck

    Tap Create a deck on the home page or navigate to /create-deck.

  2. 2 Add YouTube videos or import a playlist

    Paste YouTube video URLs one by one, or paste a YouTube playlist URL to import all tracks at once (up to 300 videos).

  3. 3 Share and play

    After creating the deck, share the link or QR code with your friends. Anyone with the link can load and play the deck — no account needed.

Tips & troubleshooting

  • Camera not working? Make sure the browser has camera permission in your device settings (Settings → Safari/Chrome → Camera).
  • Music not playing on iPhone? Always tap the "Tap to Play" overlay directly — iOS requires a user tap to allow audio. If Spotify doesn't play in-browser, it will open in the Spotify app instead.
  • Wrong song matched? musicguessr uses YouTube search to find the track. Occasionally a cover or live version may appear. You can use the "Fallback to remixes" option in settings.
  • QR code not scanning? Ensure good lighting and hold the card steady. The scanner works at around 20–60 cm from the screen.
