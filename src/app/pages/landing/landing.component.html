Common questions

Can I play Hitster without Spotify?
Yes. Choose YouTube when starting a game — it's free and works with no login. Spotify and Apple Music are also supported if you have a subscription.
Does it work on iPhone?
Yes. musicguessr is optimised for iOS Safari and Android Chrome. The camera scanner and YouTube player both work on mobile.
Do I need to download an app?
No. It runs entirely in your browser. You can optionally install it as a PWA from your browser menu for quick access.
